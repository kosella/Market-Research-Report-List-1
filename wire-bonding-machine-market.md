<p><p>A wire bonding machine is a specialized device used in the semiconductor manufacturing industry to connect integrated circuits (ICs) or chips to the external leads of a package. It utilizes a small diameter wire made of gold, aluminum, or copper to make the electrical connection between the chip and the package. This process ensures reliable electrical and mechanical connection, making wire bonding one of the most widely used packaging technologies in the semiconductor industry.</p><p>The wire bonding machine market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for consumer electronics, automotive electronics, and high-performance computing. The growth of the semiconductor industry, particularly in emerging economies, is also contributing to market expansion.
