<p><p>Leak detection for the oil and gas industry involves various market types such as flow meters, infra-red detection systems, and fiber optic sensors. Flow meters are devices used to measure the flow rate of liquids or gases, helping to identify any abnormalities that may indicate a leak. Infra-red detection systems use thermal imaging technology to detect hydrocarbon gas leaks based on temperature differences. Fiber optic sensors, on the other hand, use light propagation and reflection to identify leaks by detecting changes in vibrations or pressure within pipelines.</p></p>
