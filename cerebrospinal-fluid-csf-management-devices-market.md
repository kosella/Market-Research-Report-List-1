<p><strong>The Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management Devices Market includes two main types: CSF Shunts and CSF Drainage Systems. </p><p>CSF Shunts refer to devices implanted in the brain or spine to redirect the flow of cerebrospinal fluid. They are used to treat conditions such as hydrocephalus by allowing excess fluid to drain away from the brain.</p><p>CSF Drainage Systems, on the other hand, are external devices that help in draining the excess fluid from the brain. They are typically used in cases where a shunt is not necessary or as a temporary measure before shunt placement.</p></p>
