<p><strong>The Medical Orthopedics Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Separate System</li><li>Combining System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical orthopedics robots are classified into two types: separate system and combining system. The separate system consists of individual robotic devices designed to perform specific tasks, such as bone cutting or joint replacement. Each module operates independently and requires human intervention to set up and coordinate their actions. On the other hand, the combining system integrates different robotic devices into a single platform, allowing for synchronized and collaborative movement. These systems enhance surgical precision and efficiency, offering improved patient outcomes and reducing the risk of complications.</p></p>
