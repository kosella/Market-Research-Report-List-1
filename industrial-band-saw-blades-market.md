<p><strong>The Industrial Band Saw Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bimetal</li><li>Carbide Tipped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial band saw blades market is categorized into two main types: bimetal and carbide tipped. Bimetal blades consist of two layers, a high-speed steel tooth edge for cutting and a flexible alloy steel back for support. They offer great durability and versatility for cutting through a wide range of materials, including metal and wood. On the other hand, carbide tipped blades have carbide teeth brazed onto a steel body. This type excels in cutting tough materials such as stainless steel and exotic alloys due to its superior hardness and heat resistance.</p></p>
