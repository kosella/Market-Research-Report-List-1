<p><p>The automotive piezo buzzer components market comprises two main types: active piezo buzzers and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers generate sound using an input signal, requiring an external power source to produce sound output. On the other hand, passive piezo buzzers create sound without any external power supply and offer simplicity in their design. Both types serve various automotive applications, such as indication alerts, warning signals, and notification systems, catering to different requirements based on their distinct characteristics and functionality.</p></p>
