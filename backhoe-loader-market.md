<p><ul><li>Articulated Backhoe Loader</li><li>Rigidity Backhoe Loader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backhoe loader market consists of two main types: articulated backhoe loaders and rigidity backhoe loaders. Articulated backhoe loaders are designed with a flexible joint between the front and rear sections, allowing for better maneuverability and operation in tight spaces. On the other hand, rigidity backhoe loaders have a solid structure and provide enhanced stability and digging capabilities. Both types serve various construction and excavation purposes, but their key differentiating factor lies in their joint design and usage characteristics.</p></p>
