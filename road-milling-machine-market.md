<p><p>Road milling machines are used for removing the surface layer of roads, making them smoother and preparing them for resurfacing or construction. There are two main types of road milling machines: wheeled and tracked. Wheeled road milling machines are equipped with wheels, allowing for greater mobility and flexibility, making them suitable for smaller road projects. Tracked road milling machines, on the other hand, have tracks instead of wheels, offering better stability and traction, making them ideal for larger road projects or uneven terrains. Both types play a vital role in road construction and maintenance.</p></p>
