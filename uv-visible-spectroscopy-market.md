<p><ul><li>Single-beam Systems</li><li>Dual-beam Systems</li><li>Array-based Systems</li><li>Handheld Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV & Visible Spectroscopy market consists of different types of systems used for analyzing the electromagnetic spectrum in the UV and visible range. Single-beam systems measure the intensity of light before and after passing through a sample. Dual-beam systems utilize two light paths, allowing for better accuracy. Array-based systems use an array of detectors to simultaneously measure multiple wavelengths. Handheld systems offer portability for on-site analysis. These systems cater to various applications in fields such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
