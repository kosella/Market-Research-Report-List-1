<p><p>Application Performance Management (APM) software market can be classified into three types based on deployment models: on-premises, cloud, and hybrid. On-premises APM involves the installation and operation of the software within the organization's infrastructure. Cloud-based APM utilizes software hosted and managed by third-party service providers, offering scalability and flexibility. Hybrid APM combines both on-premises and cloud deployment, allowing organizations to maintain control over sensitive data while also leveraging the benefits of cloud computing. Organizations can choose the type that best fits their requirements and infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
