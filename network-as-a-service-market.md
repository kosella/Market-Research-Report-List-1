<p><p>Network as a Service (NaaS) market includes LAN-as-a-Service (LANaaS) and WAN-as-a-Service (WANaaS). LANaaS provides businesses with virtual Local Area Network (LAN) connectivity, enabling them to utilize network resources without the need for physical infrastructure. This allows for improved scalability and flexibility. On the other hand, WANaaS offers businesses the ability to outsource their Wide Area Network (WAN) connectivity requirements, providing secure and reliable connectivity between various geographically dispersed locations. Both LANaaS and WANaaS provide cost-effective and efficient network solutions for businesses, eliminating the need for complex network management.</p></p>
