<p><p>Fuel cell breathalyzers are devices used to measure the blood alcohol content (BAC) of an individual by analyzing their breath sample. These devices employ fuel cell technology to convert the alcohol in the breath into an electric current, which is then used to calculate the BAC level. They are commonly used by law enforcement agencies, workplaces, and individuals to ascertain the sobriety of a person.</p><p>The fuel cell breathalyzers market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. The increasing awareness about the dangers of drunk driving, stringent regulations imposed by governments regarding alcohol consumption and driving, and the rise in alcohol-related accidents are some of the key factors driving the market growth.
