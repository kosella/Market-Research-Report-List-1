<p><p>The hydraulic drum crusher market is divided into two types: electric and pneumatic. The electric drum crushers are powered by electricity, making them suitable for indoor use in industrial facilities where electricity is readily available. On the other hand, pneumatic drum crushers are powered by compressed air, making them ideal for outdoor or remote locations where electricity may not be easily accessible. Both types of drum crushers are used to flatten and dispose of empty metal drums, thus saving storage space and facilitating recycling efforts in various industries.</p></p>
