<p><p>Closed MRI systems are a type of medical imaging technology used to examine the inside of the body. The market for these systems can be categorized into four types based on the strength of the magnetic field they produce: Low-To-Mid Field, High Field, Very High Field, and Ultra-High Field. Low-To-Mid Field systems generate a magnetic field strength ranging from 0.2 to 0.5 Tesla, while High Field systems produce 1.5 to 3 Tesla. Very High Field systems generate magnetic fields ranging from 4 to 7 Tesla, and Ultra-High Field systems produce magnetic fields above 7 Tesla.</p></p>
