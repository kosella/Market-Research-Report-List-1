<p><strong>The High Speed Handpieces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Speed Handpieces are dental tools used by dental professionals for various procedures. Inside Exhaust Handpieces have an integrated exhaust system, wherein the air and debris are expelled internally through the handpiece. This design reduces the risk of cross-contamination and provides a cleaner environment. On the other hand, Outside Exhaust Handpieces feature an external exhaust system, expelling air and debris outside the handpiece through a separate tube. While this design may require additional equipment, it allows for better visibility and lighter handpiece weight, enhancing maneuverability during dental procedures.</p></p>
