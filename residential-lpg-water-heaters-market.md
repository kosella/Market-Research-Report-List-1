<p><p>The residential LPG water heaters market consists of two main types: tankless LPG water heaters and tank LPG water heaters. Tankless LPG water heaters, also known as instantaneous or demand-type heaters, provide hot water on demand without the need for a storage tank. They heat water directly, resulting in energy savings and a continuous supply of hot water. On the other hand, tank LPG water heaters store heated water in a tank for later use. They are suitable for households with high hot water consumption and provide a constant supply of hot water.</p></p>
