<p><p>POE (Point of Entry) water filters are designed to filter water as it enters a building. The market for POE water filters is divided into three main types. Reverse Osmosis POE Water Filters use a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities, including bacteria and heavy metals, from the water. Ultrafiltration Membrane POE Water Filters use a similar membrane but with larger pores, allowing them to remove larger particles while retaining essential minerals. The "Others" market refers to various other types of POE water filters, including carbon filters and ceramic filters, which employ different filtration methods to purify water.</p></p>
