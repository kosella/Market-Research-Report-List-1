<p><p>The auto chassis dyno market includes two main types: single roller type and multi-roller type. In the single roller type, a single roller is used to measure the power output of a vehicle's engine. This type is often less expensive and simpler in design. On the other hand, the multi-roller type consists of multiple rollers, allowing for more accurate measurements of power distribution among the vehicle's wheels. This type is often favored by automotive enthusiasts and professionals due to its higher precision and ability to simulate real-world driving conditions.</p></p>
