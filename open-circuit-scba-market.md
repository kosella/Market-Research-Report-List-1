<p><p>Open-circuit SCBA systems are available in single oxygen cylinder or double oxygen cylinder configurations. Single oxygen cylinder systems have one cylinder providing a limited air supply, suitable for shorter-duration use. Double oxygen cylinder systems have two cylinders for extended air supply, making them suitable for longer-duration operations. Both types offer breathing protection to users in hazardous environments, with the main difference being the duration of air supply they can provide.</p></p>
