<p><p>Green tea is available in various types based on the processing method. Fired green tea is dried by exposing leaves directly to fire. Sun-dried green tea is dried naturally under the sun. These different processing methods contribute to the diverse flavors and characteristics of green tea, providing consumers with a range of options in the market.</p></p>
