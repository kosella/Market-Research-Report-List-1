<p><p>RF MEMS (Radio Frequency Micro-electro-mechanical Systems) is a technology that combines radio frequency circuits and micro-electromechanical systems. This growth is primarily driven by the increasing demand for smartphones, Internet of Things (IoT) devices, and wireless communication technologies. With the increasing demand for smaller and more compact wireless devices, manufacturers are focusing on developing smaller RF MEMS devices without compromising their performance.
