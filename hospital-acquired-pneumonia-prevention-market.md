<p><p>Hospital-acquired pneumonia (HAP) refers to the development of pneumonia in individuals who have been hospitalized for at least 48 hours and were not incubating the infection at the time of admission. Therefore, various prevention strategies and interventions have been implemented to minimize the risk of HAP and improve patient outcomes.</p><p>The market for hospital-acquired pneumonia prevention has been experiencing significant growth in recent years.
