<p><p>Ring layer gyroscopes are inertial sensors used to measure angular velocity or rotation rate in various applications. The market for ring layer gyroscopes can be categorized into three types: 1-Axis, 2-Axis, and 3-Axis. </p><p>1-Axis gyroscopes measure rotation around a single axis, typically used in simple applications where only one-dimensional rotation needs to be measured. 2-Axis gyroscopes measure rotation around two perpendicular axes, providing more accurate measurements for applications such as navigation systems. 3-Axis gyroscopes measure rotation around all three axes, offering the highest level of accuracy and versatility, often utilized in advanced navigation systems, robotics, and virtual reality devices.</p></p>
