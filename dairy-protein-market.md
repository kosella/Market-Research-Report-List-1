Whey protein concentrate and isolate are derived from whey and have high protein content. Milk protein concentrate and isolate are obtained from milk and contain a combination of whey and casein proteins. Casein is a slow-digesting protein found in milk, while casein salts are the mineral forms of casein. These dairy protein types cater to different nutritional needs and are used in various food and beverage applications.</p></p>
