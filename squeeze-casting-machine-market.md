<p><strong>The Squeeze Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HotChamberSqueezeCastingMachine</li><li>ColdChamberSqueezeCastingMachine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Squeeze casting machine market includes hot chamber squeeze casting machines and cold chamber squeeze casting machines. Hot chamber squeeze casting machines are used for materials with low melting points, like zinc or magnesium. The machine is immersed in a molten metal bath, and a plunger injects the metal into the mold cavity. Cold chamber squeeze casting machines are used for high melting point materials, like aluminum or copper. The molten metal is poured into a separate chamber, and then a plunger moves the metal into the mold cavity.</p></p>
