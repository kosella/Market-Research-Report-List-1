<p><p>The brain tumor diagnosis and therapeutics market offers various types of tests and procedures for accurate identification and treatment planning. MRI and CT scan use imaging technology to visualize the brain and detect abnormalities. Tissue sampling involves extracting a small piece of the tumor for analysis. PET-CT scan combines positron emission tomography and computed tomography to evaluate the tumor's metabolic activity and location. Cerebral arteriogram examines blood vessels in the brain. Lumbar puncture involves analyzing cerebrospinal fluid. Molecular testing allows for genetic analysis, and EEG measures brain activity.</p></p>
