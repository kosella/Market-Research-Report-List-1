<p><strong>The Full Colour LED Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>COB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Color LED Displays can be categorized into two types: Surface-Mount Device (SMD) and Chip-on-Board (COB).</p><p>SMD displays are made up of tiny individual LEDs mounted on a circuit board. These LEDs can emit different colors, allowing for high-resolution and vibrant displays. SMD technology is widely used in outdoor advertising, stadiums, and large displays due to its flexibility and easy customization.</p><p>On the other hand, COB displays utilize a cluster of LED chips directly mounted on a substrate. This design ensures uniformity and consistency in color and brightness, making them suitable for indoor displays, video walls, and smaller screens. COB technology is known for its high reliability and energy efficiency.</p></p>
