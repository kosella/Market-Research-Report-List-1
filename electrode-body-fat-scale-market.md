<p><p>The Electrode Body Fat Scale market is categorized into three types based on the number of electrodes used in the scale. The four-electrode scales utilize four electrodes to send a low-intensity electrical current through the body, measures the resistance, and calculates body composition. Similarly, eight-electrode scales use eight electrodes for a more accurate assessment of body fat percentage, muscle mass, and water content. The "others" category refers to any other type of body fat scale that may use a different number of electrodes or have additional features not found in four or eight-electrode scales.</p></p>
