These signals are continuous and vary in voltage or current. On the other hand, in the Digital Signal Market, valve positioners receive and interpret digital signals, which are discrete and represent specific values, such as on/off commands. This enables precise and accurate control of valve position based on digital control system inputs.</p></p>
