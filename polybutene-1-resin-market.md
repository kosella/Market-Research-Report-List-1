This type of resin is known for its excellent mechanical properties, high temperature stability, and resistance to chemicals. </p><p>On the other hand, random copolymer type Polybutene-1 resin is formed by polymerizing two different monomers, resulting in a polymer chain with a random arrangement of both monomers. This type of resin offers improved flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring high toughness and elasticity.</p></p>
