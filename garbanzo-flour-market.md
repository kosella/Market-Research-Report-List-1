<p><p>Garbanzo flour, also known as chickpea flour, is available in two main types: sprouted bean flour and unsprouted garbanzo flour. Sprouted bean flour is made from garbanzo beans that have been soaked and allowed to germinate, enhancing their nutritional profile and digestibility. It is known for its higher protein and fiber content. On the other hand, unsprouted garbanzo flour is made from dried chickpeas and has a nuttier flavor and a slightly denser texture. Both types are commonly used in cooking and baking as gluten-free alternatives to regular wheat flour.</p></p>
