<p><strong>The Surgical and Exam Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The surgical and exam lighting market consists of two main types: LED and Halogen. LED lighting utilizes light-emitting diodes, providing high-intensity and efficient illumination. They offer longer lifespan, low energy consumption, and better control over brightness and color temperature. On the other hand, halogen lighting employs tungsten filaments to emit light. They are known for their low initial cost and good color rendering, but have shorter lifespan and consume more energy compared to LEDs. Both types play a significant role in providing appropriate lighting for surgical procedures and examinations.</p></p>
