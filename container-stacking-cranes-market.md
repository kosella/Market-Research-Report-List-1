<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Rubber Tyred Gantry Cranes (RTG) and Rail Mounted Gantry Crane are two types of container stacking cranes commonly used in ports and container terminals. RTGs are equipped with rubber tires, allowing them to move freely within the terminal and stack containers in a specific area. On the other hand, Rail Mounted Gantry Cranes are fixed on rails and can move along the tracks to stack containers throughout the terminal. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently handling containers and optimizing space utilization in port operations.</p></p>
