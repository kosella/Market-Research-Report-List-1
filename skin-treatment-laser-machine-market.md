<p><p>The Skin Treatment Laser Machine market is categorized into two types: Ablative Lasers and Non-Ablative Lasers. Ablative lasers are more invasive and remove the outer layers of the skin, promoting the growth of new, healthier skin. They are commonly used for skin resurfacing and treating wrinkles, scars, and pigmentation issues. On the other hand, non-ablative lasers are less invasive and target the deeper layers of the skin to stimulate collagen production and improve skin tone and texture. They are typically used for skin rejuvenation and tightening.</p></p>
