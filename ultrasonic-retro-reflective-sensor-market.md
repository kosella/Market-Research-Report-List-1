<p><p>An Ultrasonic Retro-reflective Sensor is a type of sensor that uses ultrasonic sound waves to detect objects or obstacles. These sensors emit sound waves and detect the time it takes for the waves to bounce back, allowing them to measure the distance between the sensor and the object. Unlike regular retro-reflective sensors that use infrared technology, ultrasonic retro-reflective sensors do not require a reflector to detect objects.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Retro-reflective Sensor Market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period.
