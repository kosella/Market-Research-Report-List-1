<p><strong>The Microwave Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Microwave Devices</li><li>Passive Microwave Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave devices market can be divided into two types: active microwave devices and passive microwave devices. Active microwave devices generate and amplify microwave signals, such as microwave transistors and microwave integrated circuits. Passive microwave devices, on the other hand, do not generate or amplify signals but rather manipulate and control them. Examples of passive microwave devices include microwave filters, attenuators, and couplers. Both active and passive microwave devices play a crucial role in various applications such as communication systems, radar systems, and satellite communication.</p></p>
