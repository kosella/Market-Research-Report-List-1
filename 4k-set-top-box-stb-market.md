<p><p>The 4K Set Top Box (STB) market is divided into four types based on the technology used to deliver content. Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) delivers TV signals via internet protocol, while Satellite Television uses geostationary satellites to transmit signals. Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) delivers TV signals through ground-based transmission towers. Internet Television Set-top Box (OTT) delivers content through the internet without a traditional TV provider. Hybrid Television Set-top Box combines multiple technologies to provide a wide range of content options.</p></p>
