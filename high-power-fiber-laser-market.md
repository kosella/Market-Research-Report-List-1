<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high power fiber laser market consists of two main types: continuous wave (CW) fiber laser and pulsed fiber laser. A continuous wave fiber laser emits a continuous laser beam, and is commonly used for applications requiring high precision and stability. On the other hand, a pulsed fiber laser emits laser pulses with high peak power and short durations, making it suitable for applications such as material processing and laser marking. These types of fiber lasers play a significant role in various industries including manufacturing, healthcare, telecommunications, and research.</p></p>
