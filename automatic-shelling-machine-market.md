<p><p>The automatic shelling machine market consists of two main types: vertical type and horizontal type shelling machines. Vertical type shelling machines are designed with a vertical structure, enabling the shells to be separated from the nuts through a gravity-driven process. On the other hand, horizontal type shelling machines have a horizontal structure, using a combination of mechanical and pneumatic systems to remove the shells. Both types have their own advantages and can be utilized in various industries that require efficient and automated nut shelling processes.</p></p>
