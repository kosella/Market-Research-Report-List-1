<p><p>The pedestal drill market consists of two main types: floor pedestal drills and benchtop pedestal drills. Floor pedestal drills are larger machines that are firmly mounted to the floor, providing stability and allowing for heavy-duty drilling applications. On the other hand, benchtop pedestal drills are smaller machines that can be placed on a worktable or bench, making them more compact and portable. Both types of machines are commonly used in woodworking, metalworking, and construction industries for drilling precise holes in various materials.</p></p>
