<p><ul><li>Rigid Caster</li><li>Swivel Caster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic caster market consists of two types: rigid casters and swivel casters. Rigid casters have a fixed axle, allowing movement in only one direction. These are commonly used in applications where straight-line movement is required. On the other hand, swivel casters have a rotating axle that enables multidirectional movement. Swivel casters are suitable for applications that require maneuverability in various directions. Both types play a significant role in the pneumatic caster market, catering to different needs and preferences.</p></p>
