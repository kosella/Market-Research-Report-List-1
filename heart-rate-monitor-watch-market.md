<p><ul><li>Strapless Heart Rate Monitor</li><li>Chest Strap Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart rate monitor watch market types can be divided into two main categories: strapless heart rate monitors and chest strap monitors. Strapless heart rate monitors use optical sensors on the watch to measure the wearer's heart rate directly from their wrist. On the other hand, chest strap monitors consist of a strap worn around the chest that detects the heart rate and transmits the data to the watch. Both types offer convenient and accurate heart rate monitoring options for individuals engaging in various physical activities.</p></p>
