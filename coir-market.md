<p><p>Coir market types refer to the two main categories of coir fibers available: brown and white. Brown fiber, also known as mature coir, is extracted from fully ripe coconuts. It is widely used in products like mattresses, brushes, and ropes due to its strength and durability. White fiber, on the other hand, is obtained from young green coconuts. It is soft, fine, and primarily used in manufacturing textiles and geotextiles. Both types have distinct properties that make them suitable for various industries, making the coir market versatile and diverse.</p></p>
