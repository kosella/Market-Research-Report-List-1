<p><p>Electronic films are thin materials used in the manufacturing of electronic devices. Polymer films, made of organic materials, offer flexibility and are used in displays, touch screens, and solar cells. ITO on glass refers to indium tin oxide coatings on glass, providing transparent conductivity for LCDs. PET-ITO comprises a polymer film with an indium tin oxide coating, commonly used in flexible displays. Metal mesh films have fine conductive metal grids for touch screens. Other film types include OLED films, quantum dot films, and reflective films, each serving specific purposes in electronic device production.</p></p>
