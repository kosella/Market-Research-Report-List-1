<p><p>A plastic pancreatic and biliary stent is a medical device used to open up blocked bile or pancreatic ducts in patients suffering from various conditions, such as gallstones or pancreatic cancer. These stents are typically made from medical-grade plastic and are inserted during endoscopic procedures to provide relief and improve the flow of bile or pancreatic juices.</p><p>The global plastic pancreatic and biliary stent market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing prevalence of diseases related to the biliary and pancreatic system, advancements in stent technology, and the rising adoption of minimally invasive procedures.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is driven by increasing awareness among patients about the availability of effective treatment options, as well as the growing elderly population globally. The elderly population is more prone to biliary and pancreatic disorders, and with the increasing geriatric population, the demand for plastic pancreatic and biliary stents is expected to rise.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in stent designs, such as the development of self-expandable plastic stents, are further propelling market growth.
