<p><p>The fuel cell forklift market consists of various types, including PEMFC forklifts, DMFC forklifts, and others. PEMFC (Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cell) forklifts use a polymer membrane to convert hydrogen fuel and oxygen into electricity, providing clean operation with high power density. DMFC (Direct Methanol Fuel Cell) forklifts utilize a different chemical process, converting methanol fuel directly into electricity. Other types of fuel cell forklifts refer to technologies that do not fall under the PEMFC or DMFC categories, such as alkaline fuel cells or solid oxide fuel cells, which have their own unique mechanisms for converting fuel into electricity.</p></p>
