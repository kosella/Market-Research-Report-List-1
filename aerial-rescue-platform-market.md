<p><ul><li>Elevating Platform Vehicles</li><li>Scissor Lifts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerial rescue platform market consists of two main types: elevating platform vehicles and scissor lifts. Elevating platform vehicles refer to vehicles equipped with a hydraulic or mechanical arm that can extend vertically to reach high places for rescue operations, maintenance, or firefighting. On the other hand, scissor lifts are mobile elevated platforms that use crisscrossing metal supports to extend vertically. They are commonly used in construction and industrial settings for reaching heights that are not easily accessible by ladders or scaffolding.</p></p>
