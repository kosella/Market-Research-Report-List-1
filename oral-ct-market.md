<p><p>In the oral CT market, there are two main types: floor-mounted and wall-mounted. The floor-mounted CT scanners are large machines that are securely placed on the floor, providing stability and versatility in positioning patients of different sizes. They are commonly found in hospitals and clinics where a wide range of examinations are performed. On the other hand, wall-mounted CT scanners are smaller and more compact devices that can be conveniently attached to the wall. They are often used in limited spaces or specialized settings where mobility and space-saving are important factors.</p></p>
