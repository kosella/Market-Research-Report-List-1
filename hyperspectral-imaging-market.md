Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) includes the mid-infrared range, and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) focuses on longer infrared wavelengths. "Others" category encompasses any additional specialized wavelength ranges used in hyperspectral imaging applications.</p></p>
