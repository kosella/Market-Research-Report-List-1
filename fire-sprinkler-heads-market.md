Stent sprinkler heads have a metal bracket that helps distribute water evenly. Glass bubble sprinkler heads have a glass bulb filled with a liquid that expands when heated, triggering the sprinkler. Fusible alloy sprinkler heads contain a metal alloy that melts at a specific temperature. Heat sink sprinkler heads have a heat-responsive element that activates the sprinkler when exposed to high temperatures.</p></p>
