<p><p>Industrial induction melting equipment is used for the melting and casting of various metals, such as iron, steel, aluminum, and copper, in industries such as foundries, automotive, aerospace, and construction. This equipment utilizes the principle of electromagnetic induction to generate heat, which is then transferred to the metal for melting. It offers advantages like fast and efficient melting, precise temperature control, reduced energy consumption, and improved productivity.</p><p>The industrial induction melting equipment market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key drivers for this growth is the increasing demand for metal casting in various industries.
