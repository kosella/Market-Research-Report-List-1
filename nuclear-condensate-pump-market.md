<p><p>The nuclear condensate pump market consists of various types of components such as electric motors, impellers, shafts (rotors), shaft seal packages, bearings, pump casings, and auxiliary systems. Electric motors are used to power the pump and generate the rotational motion required. Impellers are responsible for creating the pumping action by pushing the fluid. Shafts (rotors) transmit the rotational motion from the motor to the impellers. Shaft seal packages ensure that no fluid leaks from the pump. Bearings support the rotating shafts and reduce friction. Pump casings enclose the components and guide the flow of fluid. Auxiliary systems provide additional support functions for the pump's operation.</p></p>
