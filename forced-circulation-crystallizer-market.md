<p><strong>The Forced Circulation Crystallizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Vapor Recompression (TVR) Evaporation System</li><li>Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) Evaporation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forced circulation crystallizer market types include Thermal Vapor Recompression (TVR) Evaporation System and Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) Evaporation System. In TVR systems, steam is used to evaporate the liquid, and the resulting vapor is compressed to increase its temperature and pressure, promoting crystallization. MVR systems, on the other hand, utilize mechanical energy to compress the vapor, achieving the same effect. Both systems offer efficient and cost-effective solutions for crystallization processes in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
