<p><p>Oxidative stress analysis refers to the measurement and assessment of oxidative stress levels in individuals. Oxidative stress occurs when there is an imbalance between the production of reactive oxygen species (ROS) and the body's ability to detoxify them or repair the resulting damage. <p><p>The oxidative stress analysis market offers various types of techniques to assess oxidative stress levels in cells and tissues. Microscopy allows visualization of ROS production and localization. High-content screening combines microscopy and image analysis software for detailed analysis of oxidative stress-related parameters. Other techniques include ELISA, PCR, and spectrophotometry. These methods help researchers understand oxidative stress-related diseases and develop new therapies to mitigate its harmful effects.</p></p>
