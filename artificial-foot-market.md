<p><ul><li>Mechanical Foot</li><li>Microprocessor Foot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artificial foot market types include mechanical foot and microprocessor foot. A mechanical foot is a type of prosthetic foot that operates using mechanical components, such as springs and joints, to simulate natural movement. These feet are often more affordable and ideal for individuals with a relatively low activity level. On the other hand, microprocessor feet use advanced technology to adapt in real-time to changing conditions, providing a more responsive and natural walking experience. These feet are generally more expensive but offer improved stability and flexibility for individuals with higher activity levels.</p></p>
