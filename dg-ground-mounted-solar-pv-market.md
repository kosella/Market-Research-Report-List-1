<p><p>The DG ground-mounted solar PV market consists of two main types: crystalline silicon and thin film. Crystalline silicon panels utilize more established technology and are made up of silicon wafers. They are known for their efficiency and durability. On the other hand, thin film panels are made by depositing thin layers of semiconducting material onto a substrate. While they are less efficient and durable compared to crystalline silicon, they are typically cheaper to produce. Both types contribute to the growing market of ground-mounted solar PV systems.</p></p>
