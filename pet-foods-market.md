<p><p>Pet foods are specially formulated and dietary products that are intended for the consumption of domesticated animals, particularly pets such as dogs, cats, birds, and fish. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing adoption of pets, changing lifestyles, and growing awareness about pet health and nutrition.
