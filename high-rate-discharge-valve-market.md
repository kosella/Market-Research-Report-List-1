<p><p>The High Rate Discharge Valve market consists of two main types: Automatic and Manual. </p><p>Automatic valves are designed to release fluids or gases automatically when specific conditions are met, such as high pressure or temperature. These valves are commonly used in industrial settings where fast and reliable discharge is required to ensure safety and prevent equipment damage.</p><p>On the other hand, Manual valves require human intervention to open or close the valve.
