<p><ul><li>Disposable</li><li>Reusable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jet injector market can be categorized into two types: disposable and reusable. Disposable jet injectors are designed for single-use only and are discarded after each administration. They offer convenience and eliminate the risk of cross-contamination. On the other hand, reusable jet injectors can be used multiple times after proper sterilization. They are more cost-effective in the long run but require maintenance and sterilization procedures. Both types provide a needle-free injection method, making them suitable for various medical applications.</p></p>
