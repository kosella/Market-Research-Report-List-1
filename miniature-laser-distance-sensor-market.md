<p><p>The Miniature Laser Distance Sensor market comprises two types: Long Distance Type and Reflection Type. The Long Distance Type sensors are designed to measure distances over larger ranges, typically up to several meters. These sensors emit laser beams and measure the time it takes for the beam to bounce back to calculate the distance. On the other hand, Reflection Type sensors are capable of detecting objects at close range by analyzing the reflections of laser beams. Both types are compact in size and find applications in various industries for distance measurement purposes.</p></p>
