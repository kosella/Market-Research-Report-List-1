<p><p>The protective cabinets market includes two main types: outdoor cabinets and indoor cabinets. Outdoor cabinets are designed to withstand various weather conditions and provide protection for equipment installed in outdoor areas, such as telecommunication gear or electrical components. They are typically made from durable materials and feature weatherproof seals. On the other hand, indoor cabinets are mainly used in controlled indoor environments to house sensitive equipment like servers, networking devices, or computer systems. They provide physical security and help regulate temperature and humidity levels to ensure optimal operations of the installed equipment.</p></p>
