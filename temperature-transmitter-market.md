<p><p>The temperature transmitter market encompasses three main types: Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD), Thermistor, and Thermocouple. RTD devices measure temperature by detecting changes in electrical resistance. Thermistors, on the other hand, use the change in electrical resistance of semiconductor materials to determine temperature. Lastly, thermocouples generate a voltage signal based on the temperature difference between two junctions. These devices are widely used in various industries to accurately measure and transmit temperature data for monitoring and control purposes.</p></p>
