<p><p>The medical crutches market consists of various types of crutches that aid in mobility for individuals with temporary or permanent disabilities. Underarm crutches are the most common type, which go under the armpits for support. Forearm crutches provide support using a cuff around the forearm. Tetrapod crutches have four legs for enhanced stability. Leg support crutches provide support to one leg while keeping the injured leg lifted. Platform crutches have a platform to rest the forearm on. Others include knee crutches, spring-assisted crutches, and ergonomic crutches, offering different features for specific needs.</p></p>
