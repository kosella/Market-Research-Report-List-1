<p><p>Enteral feeding formulas refer to liquid nutritional products that are specially formulated to provide essential nutrients to patients who are unable to consume food orally. Enteral feeding formulas play a crucial role in the management of various medical conditions such as malnutrition, gastrointestinal disorders, cancer, and critical illnesses.</p><p>The enteral feeding formulas market has been experiencing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, increasing geriatric population, and the growing demand for home-based healthcare services.
