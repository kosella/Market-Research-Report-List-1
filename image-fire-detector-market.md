<p><ul><li>Ionization Detector Type</li><li>Photoelectric Detector Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Image fire detectors are devices used to detect fires in various settings. Three common types of image fire detectors in the market are ionization detector type, photoelectric detector type, and others. Ionization detectors work by ionizing air particles and detecting changes in the electrical current caused by smoke. Photoelectric detectors use a light source and a light sensor to detect smoke particles. The "others" category includes specialty detectors such as flame detectors and heat detectors, which use different sensors to detect fire. These detectors are essential for ensuring early detection and prompt action in case of a fire.</p></p>
