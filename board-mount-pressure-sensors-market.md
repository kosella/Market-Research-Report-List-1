<p><p>The board mount pressure sensors market is categorized into various types based on their output. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals proportional to the pressure measured. Digital output sensors convert the pressure into digital data for easy handling by microcontrollers or digital devices. RF/Wireless output sensors transmit the pressure data wirelessly to external receivers or systems, eliminating the need for physical connections. Others in this market include sensors with I2C or SPI interface outputs, frequency outputs, or pressure switches with binary outputs.</p></p>
