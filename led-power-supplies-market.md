<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>LED power supplies market can be categorized into two types - internal drivers and external drivers. Internal drivers are built-in power supplies that are directly integrated into the LED lighting fixture. They provide a compact and cost-effective solution, but offer limited flexibility in terms of brightness control. On the other hand, external drivers are separate devices that are connected to the LED lighting fixture externally. They offer greater flexibility in terms of brightness control and are preferred in applications where dimming or color-changing capabilities are required. Both types have their own advantages and are used depending on the specific requirements of the lighting application.</p></p>
