<p><p>The automotive closed die forgings market includes three types: custom forging, captive forging, and catalog forging. Custom forging involves designing and manufacturing forgings based on specific customer requirements, offering a high level of customization. Captive forging refers to forgings produced in-house by automotive manufacturers to meet their own production needs. Catalog forging involves producing forgings based on standardized designs and specifications, which are then available for multiple customers to purchase. Each type caters to different customer demands and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
