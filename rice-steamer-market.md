<p><p>The Rice Steamer market comprises three types of cookers; Conventional Rice Cooker, Micom Rice Cooker, and IH Rice Cooker. Conventional Rice Cookers are basic and affordable, using heat and a timer to cook the rice. Micom Rice Cookers have microcomputer technology that adjusts cooking time and temperature for optimal results. They offer various cooking functions and are known for their versatility. IH Rice Cookers utilize electromagnetic induction to heat the inner pot directly, resulting in faster and more even cooking. They are considered high-end cookers due to their advanced features and precise temperature control.</p></p>
