<p><strong>The Thermal Printhead Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thick Film Thermal Printhead</li><li>Thin Film Thermal Printhead</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal printheads are key components in thermal printers which generate heat to produce images on thermal papers. The thick film thermal printhead technology utilizes a thick resistive layer to create heat, making it more durable and suitable for high-volume printing applications. On the other hand, thin film thermal printhead technology uses a thin layer of resistive material, resulting in more precise and high-resolution prints. Both types have their own advantages and are utilized in various industries, such as retail, healthcare, and transportation, depending on the specific printing requirements.</p></p>
