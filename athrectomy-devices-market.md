<p><p>Athrectomy Devices Market includes various types such as Directional Atherectomy, Rotational Atherectomy, Orbital Atherectomy, and Laser Atherectomy. Directional atherectomy involves the use of a catheter with a blade to cut and remove plaque. Rotational atherectomy utilizes a rotating burr to shave off plaque from the artery walls. Orbital atherectomy involves a device with a diamond-coated crown that sands away plaque. Laser atherectomy uses laser energy to vaporize and remove plaque from blood vessels. These devices are used in the treatment of atherosclerosis.</p></p>
