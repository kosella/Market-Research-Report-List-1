<p><p>In the Automotive CAN FD transceivers market, there are two types of markets: isolated and non-isolated. Isolated transceivers have electrical isolation between the CAN-Bus network and the host system, providing protection against electrical noise and voltage spikes. They are commonly used in applications where high reliability and robustness are required. On the other hand, non-isolated transceivers do not offer electrical isolation and are typically used in applications where isolation is not necessary. The choice between the two depends on the specific requirements and constraints of the automotive application.</p></p>
