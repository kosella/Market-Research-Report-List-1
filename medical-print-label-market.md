<p><p>Medical print label market has various types including Wet Glued Labels, Pressure Sensitive/Self Adhesive Labels, Liner-less labels, Multi-part Barcode Labels, In-mold labels, and Shrink Sleeve Labels. Wet glued labels are adhered using water-based adhesive, while Pressure Sensitive/Self Adhesive Labels require pressure to stick. Liner-less labels do not have a backing paper, Multi-part Barcode Labels have multiple layers, In-mold labels are molded into the product packaging, and Shrink Sleeve Labels shrink to conform to the shape of the container. Each type offers unique benefits for different applications in the medical industry.</p></p>
