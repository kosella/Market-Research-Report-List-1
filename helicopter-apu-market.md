<p><p>The helicopter APU market consists of two main types: electric APU and pneumatic APU. Electric APU refers to the use of an electric generator or motor to power the auxiliary power unit. This type is known for its efficiency and reduced emissions. On the other hand, pneumatic APU relies on compressed air to generate power for various aircraft functions. This type is preferred for its simplicity, reliability, and ease of maintenance.
