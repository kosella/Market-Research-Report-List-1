<p><p>Weather forecasting systems and solutions market is categorized into three types: satellite-based systems, ground-based systems, and airborne systems. Ground-based systems consist of weather stations and radar systems that collect data on temperature, precipitation, wind speed, and other atmospheric conditions. Airborne systems involve the use of aircraft equipped with sensors to collect real-time weather data, particularly for remote areas. These systems play a vital role in accurately predicting weather conditions and providing timely information to individuals and organizations.</p></p>
