Sugar alcohols like erythritol and xylitol are low-calorie alternatives to regular sugar and are commonly used in sugar-free products. Lastly, other functional sugars can encompass a variety of natural or artificial sweeteners designed to provide specific health benefits, such as stevia or aspartame.</p></p>
