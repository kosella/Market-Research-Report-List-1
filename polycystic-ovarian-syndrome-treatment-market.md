<p><p>Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. It is characterized by the growth of cysts on the ovaries, irregular menstrual cycles, and high levels of male hormones. The treatment for PCOS aims to manage symptoms and prevent long-term complications such as infertility, diabetes, and heart disease.</p><p>The treatment for PCOS typically involves a combination of lifestyle changes, medication, and surgery. Lifestyle changes include regular exercise, a healthy diet, and weight loss, which can help regulate menstrual cycles and improve insulin sensitivity. Medications like hormonal contraceptives, anti-androgens, and insulin-sensitizing agents are often prescribed to control symptoms such as irregular periods, excessive hair growth, and acne. In some cases, surgical interventions like ovarian drilling or in vitro fertilization may be required to improve fertility.</p><p>The Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome Treatment Market is expected to witness notable growth in the coming years.
