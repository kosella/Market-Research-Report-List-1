<p><strong>The Lab Water Baths Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Baths</li><li>Shaking Water Baths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lab water baths market comprises two main types: circulating water baths and shaking water baths. Circulating water baths are designed to maintain a constant temperature throughout the bath by circulating water and ensuring even heat distribution. They are commonly used for incubation, cell culture, and other applications requiring precise temperature control. On the other hand, shaking water baths provide controlled agitation to samples by shaking the water in the bath. This enables better mixing and faster reactions, making them ideal for applications such as chemical synthesis and molecular biology research.</p></p>
