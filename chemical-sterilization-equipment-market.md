Formaldehyde sterilization uses formaldehyde gas to decontaminate items. Other chemical sterilization methods include glutaraldehyde and chlorine dioxide sterilization. These methods are used in healthcare settings, laboratories, and pharmaceutical industries to ensure effective sterilization and prevent the spread of infections.</p></p>
