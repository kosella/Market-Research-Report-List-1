<p><p>There are two main types of Universal Testing Machines (UTM) available in the market - Electromechanical UTM and Hydraulic UTM. Electromechanical UTM operates using an electric motor to apply the required force for testing materials, while Hydraulic UTM uses hydraulic systems to apply the force. Electromechanical UTM is more commonly used for low to medium force applications, providing accurate and precise results. On the other hand, Hydraulic UTM is suitable for higher force applications, offering greater strength and durability. Both types are employed for a wide range of material testing purposes in various industries.</p></p>
