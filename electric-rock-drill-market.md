<p><p>The Electric Rock Drill market consists of two major types: Pneumatic Rock Drill and Hydraulic Rock Drill. Pneumatic Rock Drills operate using compressed air and are used for various drilling applications. On the other hand, Hydraulic Rock Drills rely on hydraulic power to function and find utility in drilling through tough surfaces like rocks and concrete. Both types offer unique advantages, such as increased power and efficiency. The Electric Rock Drill market witnesses demand for these types due to their versatility and effectiveness in various industries requiring drilling operations.</p></p>
