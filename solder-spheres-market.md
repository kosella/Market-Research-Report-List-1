<p><p>Solder spheres are used in electronic packaging to connect integrated circuits to circuit boards. Lead solder spheres contain lead as a main component and have been widely used in the past. However, due to environmental concerns, lead-free solder spheres have gained popularity. Lead-free solder spheres are made from alloys that do not contain lead, such as tin-silver-copper or tin-silver. This shift in the market reflects an increasing demand for environmentally friendly and safer alternatives, as lead can be harmful if not handled properly.</p></p>
