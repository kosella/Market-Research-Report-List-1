<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Micro Automatic Sampler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inhaled Type</li><li>Pusher Type</li><li>Monolithic Sample Ring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Automatic Sampler market includes three main types: Inhaled Type, Pusher Type, and Monolithic Sample Ring Type. The Inhaled Type sampler allows for direct sampling of aerosols or vapors by drawing them into the sampling system. The Pusher Type sampler uses a mechanical arm to push samples into the sampling system, usually activated by a button or lever. The Monolithic Sample Ring Type sampler collects samples on a solid ring material, which can then be easily removed and analyzed. These different types cater to varying sampling needs and preferences.</p></p>
