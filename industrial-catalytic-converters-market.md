<p><p>Industrial catalytic converters are devices used in industrial operations to control and reduce emissions of harmful pollutants such as nitrogen oxides, carbon monoxide, and volatile organic compounds. These converters work by converting these pollutants into less harmful substances through a chemical reaction. They are commonly used in industries such as power plants, chemical manufacturing, petroleum refineries, and automotive manufacturing.</p><p>The global market for industrial catalytic converters has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. The increasing focus on environmental regulations and the need to comply with stringent emission standards has fueled the demand for these converters.
