<p><p>The healthcare plastics market consists of various types of plastics used in the healthcare industry. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is commonly used for tubing and catheters due to its flexibility and compatibility with various fluids. PE (polyethylene) is used in packaging applications. PP (polypropylene) is used for syringes and medical containers due to its high chemical resistance. PS (polystyrene) is used for disposable labware items. ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene) is used in medical device housings. PC (polycarbonate) is used for medical equipment and face shields. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) is used for implantable devices. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) is used for medical tubing and catheters due to its biocompatibility and flexibility.</p></p>
