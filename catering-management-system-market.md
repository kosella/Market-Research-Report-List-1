<p><p>Catering Management System market is categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on the internet and accessed remotely through web browsers, allowing users to store and manage data on virtual servers. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed and operated locally on the organization's own hardware and servers. While cloud-based systems offer scalability, flexibility, and cost benefits, on-premises systems provide greater control and data security but require upfront investment and maintenance.</p></p>
