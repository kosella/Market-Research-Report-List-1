<p><p>3D and 4D ultrasound equipment are advanced medical imaging devices that use sound waves to create three-dimensional or four-dimensional images of the fetus inside the womb. The increasing awareness and preference for high-quality prenatal imaging among expecting parents is a major factor driving the market growth. The ability of 3D and 4D ultrasounds to offer detailed anatomical information about the fetus, as well as the opportunity to capture real-time movements and facial expressions, has contributed to their growing popularity.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements in ultrasound imaging, such as the development of high-resolution transducers and the integration of artificial intelligence and deep learning algorithms, have further enhanced the capabilities of 3D and 4D ultrasound equipment, driving market growth.
