<p><p>Biological safety testing service refers to the testing services conducted on various biological products to ensure their safety, purity, and compliance with regulatory standards. These services are primarily used by pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device companies to assess the safety and efficacy of their products before they are released into the market. The biological safety testing service includes various tests such as bioburden testing, endotoxin testing, sterility testing, cell line authentication, and mycoplasma testing.</p><p>The global biological safety testing service market is witnessing significant growth due to factors such as the increasing number of biologics and biosimilars in the pharmaceutical industry, rising adoption of advanced technologies in healthcare, and stringent regulations for ensuring the safety of biological products. Additionally, the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases and the growing demand for personalized medicine are also driving the demand for biological safety testing services.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology, such as the development of automated testing platforms and rapid microbiological testing methods, are expected to boost market growth.
