<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) Coating System market includes various types such as Sputtering Deposition, Evaporation Deposition, and Others. Sputtering Deposition is a PVD method where atoms are ejected from a target material and then deposit on a substrate. Evaporation Deposition involves the heating of a material in a vacuum, causing it to vaporize and then condense on a substrate. Lastly, the 'Others' category includes different PVD methods such as arc evaporation, ion plating, and laser ablation, which have their own unique processes for coating deposition.</p></p>
