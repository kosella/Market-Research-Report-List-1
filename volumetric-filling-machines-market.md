<p><p>Volumetric filling machines are used in various industries to accurately fill containers with liquid or granular products. Automatic filling machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention, and offer high-speed production capabilities. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for tasks like container placement and triggering the filling process. While automatic machines are suitable for large-scale production, semi-automatic machines are more commonly used by smaller or medium-sized businesses.</p></p>
