<p><p>Tape degaussers are devices used to erase magnetic data from tapes. They can be classified into three market types. Coil degaussers use an electromagnet to generate a strong magnetic field and erase the data. Capacitive discharge degaussers use a high-voltage surge to generate a powerful magnetic pulse. Permanent magnet degaussers use a fixed magnet to demagnetize the tapes. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications and tape formats.</p></p>
