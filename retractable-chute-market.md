<p><ul><li>Closed Retractable Chute</li><li>Open Retractable Chute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retractable chute market consists of two main types: closed retractable chutes and open retractable chutes. Closed retractable chutes are designed with a sealed system that ensures the contained material is securely transported without any exposure or spillage. On the other hand, open retractable chutes are built with an open-ended design, allowing for easy loading and unloading of materials. While closed chutes prioritize containment and safety, open chutes offer increased accessibility and convenience for material handling.</p></p>
