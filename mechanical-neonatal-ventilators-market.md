<p><p>Mechanical Neonatal Ventilators comprise Invasive Ventilators and Non-Invasive Ventilators. Non-Invasive Ventilators deliver oxygen through a mask or nasal prongs, without the need for intubation. Both types of ventilators provide respiratory support to newborn babies with breathing difficulties. The market for these ventilators is essential in providing critical care to neonates with respiratory issues.</p></p>
