<p><p>The Goji Juice market consists of two main types: Goji Juice Concentrate and Goji Raw Juice. Goji Juice Concentrate is made by extracting the juice from goji berries and removing most of the water content, resulting in a concentrated form that can be easily diluted. On the other hand, Goji Raw Juice is made by pressing the goji berries directly, without removing any water content. Both types offer the health benefits of goji berries and are popular among consumers interested in natural and nutritious beverages.</p></p>
