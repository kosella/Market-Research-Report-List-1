<p><p>The Mobile Fronthaul market consists of different types, including Cloud RAN and Centralized RAN. Cloud RAN, also known as C-RAN, is an architecture where the processing functions of baseband units are centralized in a cloud-based data center. It allows for flexible deployment and resource allocation. On the other hand, Centralized RAN refers to an architecture where the baseband units are aggregated in a central location. This approach simplifies installation and maintenance while ensuring efficient utilization of resources. Both Cloud RAN and Centralized RAN offer advantages in terms of scalability, cost reduction, and improved network performance.</p></p>
