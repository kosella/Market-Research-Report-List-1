The increasing prevalence of HAIs, the rising government initiatives to prevent and control infections, and the growing awareness among healthcare providers about the importance of infection control are some of the key factors driving market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the integration of advanced technologies like machine learning and artificial intelligence in infection surveillance systems is further propelling the market growth. These technologies enable real-time monitoring and analysis of infection data, aiding in the early detection of potential outbreaks and facilitating prompt interventions.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has led to a surge in the demand for infection surveillance systems.
