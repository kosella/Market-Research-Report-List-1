<p><p>The Laser Galvo Scanners market consists of different types: Single Axis, 2-Axis, and 3-Axis. Single Axis scanners allow laser beam deflection along one axis only, making them suitable for simple applications. 2-axis scanners can deflect the laser beam along two axes, offering more flexibility in scanning patterns and enabling more complex operations. 3-axis scanners provide the highest level of control, allowing deflection along three axes. Each type caters to different requirements, and customers choose based on their specific needs for precision, speed, and versatility in laser scanning applications.</p></p>
