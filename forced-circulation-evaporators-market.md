<p><p>Forced circulation evaporators are used to separate liquids from solids or concentrate solutions by applying thermal energy. Single-effect forced circulation evaporators use a pump to circulate the solution through the heat exchanger and evaporator at a single temperature level. Multi-effect forced circulation evaporators, on the other hand, use multiple evaporator stages arranged in series, where the vapor generated in each stage is used to heat the next stage, resulting in higher energy efficiency. These types of evaporators find application in various industries such as food and beverage, chemical, and pharmaceutical.</p></p>
