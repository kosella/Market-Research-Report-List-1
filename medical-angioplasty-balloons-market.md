Normal balloons are used for dilating narrowed blood vessels, drug-eluting balloons release medication to prevent re-narrowing, cutting balloons have small blades to cut through plaque, and scoring balloons have tiny wires to create small grooves in the plaque. Each type serves a different purpose in treating coronary artery disease and peripheral artery disease.</p></p>
