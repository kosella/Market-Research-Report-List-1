<p><p>The Continuous Wave Quantum Cascade Laser (CW-QCL) market consists of three main types: Fabry-Perot, Distributed Feedback (DFB), and Tunable External Cavities. Fabry-Perot lasers use two partially reflective mirrors to create a standing wave pattern, while DFB lasers have a grating structure that provides feedback. Tunable External Cavities lasers rely on external feedback to adjust the emitted wavelength. These different types cater to various applications and industries, offering advantages such as high power, high efficiency, and tunability in different wavelength ranges.</p></p>
