<p><ul><li>Liquid propane (LP) Barbecues</li><li>Natural gas (NG) Barbecues</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas barbecues come in two main market types: liquid propane (LP) barbecues and natural gas (NG) barbecues. LP barbecues are fueled by propane gas stored in tanks, making them portable and easy to use. They offer convenience and are popular for outdoor cooking. On the other hand, NG barbecues are connected directly to a natural gas supply and do not require the use of tanks. They are often permanent fixtures and offer cost savings in the long run, but require a dedicated gas line to operate.</p></p>
