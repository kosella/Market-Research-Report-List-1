<p><p>The ostomy products market can be divided into two main types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags are designed to be disposable and consist of a pouch and an adhesive flange that sticks directly to the skin. These bags are easy to use and suitable for individuals with active lifestyles. Two-piece bags, on the other hand, consist of a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for easy pouch changes without removing the skin barrier. They are more flexible and can be beneficial for individuals with frequent pouch changes or those needing additional skin protection.</p></p>
