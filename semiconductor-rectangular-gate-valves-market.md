<p><p>Semiconductor rectangular gate valves are a type of valves used in the semiconductor industry to control the flow of gases. They come in two market types: one cylinder and double cylinder. </p><p>One cylinder gate valves feature a single actuation cylinder, which moves the gate to open or close the valve. This design is simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for applications with low pressure and flow requirements.</p><p>Double cylinder gate valves, on the other hand, have two actuation cylinders. This design provides enhanced sealing and high-pressure capabilities, making them suitable for applications demanding greater performance and reliability.</p></p>
