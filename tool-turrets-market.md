<p><p>Tool turrets market types include disc-type, head-type, and crown type. The disc-type turret is designed with a rotating disc that holds multiple tools, making it versatile for various machining operations. Head-type turrets have a stationary tool holder and a moving slide that brings the tools into position. Crown type turrets have a unique design with a crown-shaped disc, offering more compactness and increased rigidity. These different market types cater to different machining needs, providing flexibility, efficiency, and stability in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
