<p><strong>The Semiconductor Test Sorter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity-Feed Test Sorter</li><li>Turret Test Sorter</li><li>Pick-and-Place Test Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor test sorter market consists of three main types: gravity-feed test sorter, turret test sorter, and pick-and-place test sorter. </p><p>Gravity-feed test sorters use gravity to feed the devices to the testing area, providing a continuous flow. Turret test sorters have a rotating turret that holds and aligns the devices for testing. Pick-and-place test sorters use robotic arms to pick up and place devices for testing. </p><p>These different types cater to the unique requirements of semiconductor testing, offering reliable and efficient methods to accurately sort and test semiconductor devices.</p></p>
