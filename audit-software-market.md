<p><p>Audit software can be categorized into three market types: cloud-based, installed-PC, and installed-mobile. Cloud-based audit software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it through an internet connection. Installed-PC software requires installation and is used on personal computers while being restricted to that device. Installed-mobile software is similar to installed-PC software but is designed specifically for mobile devices such as smartphones or tablets. These market types offer various options for organizations to choose from based on their preferences and needs for conducting audits efficiently.</p></p>
