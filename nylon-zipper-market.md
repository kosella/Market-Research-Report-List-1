<p><p>The nylon zipper market consists of three main types: close-end zippers, open-end zippers, and two-way zippers. Close-end zippers are commonly found in garments and bags, featuring one end that can be opened and closed. Open-end zippers, on the other hand, have two separate ends and are often used in jackets and coats. Two-way zippers have two sliders, allowing the zipper to be opened from both ends simultaneously, which is useful for items such as tents or backpacks. These different types cater to various consumer needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
