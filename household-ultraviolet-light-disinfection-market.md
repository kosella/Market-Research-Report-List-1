<p><strong>The Household Ultraviolet Light Disinfection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary cathode low pressure mercury UV disinfection</li><li>High-intensity ultraviolet disinfection</li><li>Ozone UV disinfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household ultraviolet light disinfection market consists of three types: ordinary cathode low-pressure mercury UV disinfection, high-intensity ultraviolet disinfection, and ozone UV disinfection. Ordinary cathode low-pressure mercury UV disinfection uses a low-pressure mercury lamp to emit UV light, killing bacteria and viruses. High-intensity ultraviolet disinfection utilizes a higher intensity of UV light to effectively eliminate pathogens. Ozone UV disinfection combines UV light with ozone gas to achieve a higher level of disinfection, as ozone has strong oxidative properties. These technologies offer various levels of disinfection for household applications.</p></p>
