<p><p>The screen changers market comprises two main types: manual and hydraulic. Manual screen changers require operator intervention to initiate screen changes. They are typically used in low-volume applications and are more cost-effective. On the other hand, hydraulic screen changers offer automated and continuous operation, making them suitable for high-volume production lines. They require minimal operator intervention and provide efficient and reliable screen changes. Hydraulic screen changers are known for their durability and robust performance, albeit being more expensive than manual ones.</p></p>
