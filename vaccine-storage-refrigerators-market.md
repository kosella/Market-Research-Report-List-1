<p><p>Vaccine storage refrigerators can be classified into two types: common indoor vaccine refrigerators and cold chain vaccine refrigerators. Common indoor vaccine refrigerators are typically used in healthcare facilities where vaccines are stored and administered on-site. They are designed to maintain a constant temperature range to ensure the efficacy of vaccines. Cold chain vaccine refrigerators, on the other hand, are specifically designed to maintain the required temperature during vaccine transportation and distribution. These refrigerators are crucial to ensure the integrity of vaccines throughout the cold chain supply process.</p></p>
