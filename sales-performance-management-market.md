<p><p>Sales Performance Management (SPM) refers to the process of maximizing sales team efficiency and effectiveness. There are three main types of SPM markets: </p><p>1. Web-Based SPM: This market type involves using a web platform to manage and track sales performance. It allows for real-time data access, analytics, and collaboration, enabling organizations to make informed decisions and optimize their sales processes.</p><p>2. On-Premise SPM: In this market type, companies install and operate SPM software on their own servers or data centers. It offers more control over data and customization options but requires higher IT infrastructure and maintenance costs.</p><p>3. Cloud-Based SPM: This market type utilizes cloud-based software solutions that are hosted and maintained by a third-party provider. It offers flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs while providing easy access to sales data anytime, anywhere.</p></p>
