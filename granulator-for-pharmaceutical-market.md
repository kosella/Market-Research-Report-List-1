<p><p>The granulator market for pharmaceuticals consists of two main types: dry granulators and wet granulators. Dry granulators are used to break down powders into granules without the addition of any liquid or solvent. They are ideal for compressing and compacting dry materials. On the other hand, wet granulators involve the use of a liquid or solvent to form granules. These granulators are effective for processing materials that are not easily compacted or cohesive in dry form. Both types of granulators cater to the specific needs and preferences of the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
