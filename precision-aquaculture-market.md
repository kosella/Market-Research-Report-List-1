<p><p>Precision aquaculture refers to the application of advanced technologies and data analytics in the aquaculture industry to enhance productivity, efficiency, and sustainability. Such technologies help in minimizing risks and maximizing productivity.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing focus on environmental sustainability is driving the adoption of precision aquaculture.
