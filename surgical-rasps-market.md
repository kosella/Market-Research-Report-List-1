<p><p>Surgical rasps are tools used in surgical procedures to remove bone or tissue at the surgical site. They come in different types: double-ended and single-ended rasps. Double-ended rasps have a rasp on both ends, allowing for more versatility during surgery. They can be used for different purposes without switching tools. On the other hand, single-ended rasps have a rasp on only one end, limiting their use to a specific purpose. Both types are vital in surgical settings, but the double-ended rasps offer greater flexibility during procedures.</p></p>
