<p><p>Predictive Maintenance is the practice of monitoring equipment, machinery, or systems in order to identify and prevent potential issues before they cause unexpected downtime or failure. IIoT enables real-time data collection and analysis from connected devices, allowing organizations to proactively identify and address maintenance issues.
