<p><p>A 3D Cone Beam CT (Computed Tomography) system is an innovative medical imaging solution that allows healthcare professionals to obtain detailed three-dimensional images of patients' anatomy. Unlike traditional CT scans, which require multiple X-ray images from different angles, the 3D Cone Beam CT system captures all necessary images in one rotation, providing faster and more accurate results.</p><p>The 3D Cone Beam CT System market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to expand at a CAGR of 5.90% during the forecast period.
