<p><p>Self-priming chopper pumps are widely used in various industries for handling wastewater, solids, and heavy slurry. These pumps are categorized into two main market types: vertical chopper pumps and horizontal chopper pumps. </p><p>Vertical chopper pumps are designed with a vertical shaft and impeller, allowing them to be installed in sumps or pits. They are ideal for applications where space is limited or when quick priming is required. </p><p>On the other hand, horizontal chopper pumps have a horizontal shaft and impeller, making them suitable for installations in above-ground tanks or open pits. These pumps offer excellent solids handling capabilities and are commonly used in industrial processes and municipal wastewater treatment plants.</p></p>
