<p><ul><li>Metal Halide</li><li>UHP Mercury</li><li>Xenon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The projector bulb market is primarily segmented into three types: Metal Halide, UHP Mercury, and Xenon bulbs. Metal Halide bulbs are an efficient lighting option that produce bright, high-intensity light. UHP Mercury bulbs, commonly used in DLP projectors, generate light through an electric arc passing through a mixture of mercury vapor and metal halides. Xenon bulbs, on the other hand, produce intense and pure white light, making them a popular choice for high-end projectors. Each type of bulb offers unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different projector needs.</p></p>
