<p><p>The Stable Isotope Analyzer market consists of two main types: Stable Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry (IRMS) and Laser-based. Stable IRMS utilizes mass spectrometry to measure the isotopic composition of elements in a sample. It is highly accurate and widely used in various industries like environmental and geological research. On the other hand, Laser-based analyzers use lasers to selectively excite and measure isotopes. They are compact, portable, and suitable for on-site analysis. Both types play a crucial role in determining isotopic ratios, enabling scientists and researchers to make accurate conclusions about various substances.</p></p>
