<p><strong>The Plant-based Nanocellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NFC Type</li><li>NCC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plant-based nanocellulose market consists of two main types: NFC Type and NCC Type. NFC Type refers to nanofibrillated cellulose, which is obtained through mechanical treatment of cellulose fibers. It has high aspect ratio, large surface area, and excellent mechanical properties, making it suitable for various applications like composites, coatings, and packaging materials. On the other hand, NCC Type stands for nanocrystalline cellulose, which is derived through acid hydrolysis of cellulose. It possesses unique crystalline structure and superior strength, making it ideal for applications in electronics, biomedical, and food industries.</p></p>
