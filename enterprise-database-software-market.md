<p><strong>The Enterprise Database Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Promise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise Database Software Market is divided into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based database software runs on remote servers and offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness by allowing users to access and manage the database through the internet. On-Premise software, on the other hand, is hosted locally by organizations on their own servers. It provides improved control, security, and customization options but requires specific hardware and maintenance. The choice depends on factors like data sensitivity, budget, and IT infrastructure preferences of businesses.</p></p>
