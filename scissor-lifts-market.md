<p><p>The scissor lift market consists of three main types: hydraulic, pneumatic, and mechanical. Hydraulic scissor lifts rely on hydraulic cylinders to raise and lower the platform, offering smooth and precise movements. Pneumatic scissor lifts use a pneumatic system to lift the platform, suitable for environments where electricity is not available or considered hazardous. Mechanical scissor lifts are manually operated, utilizing a mechanical mechanism to elevate the platform. Each variant serves distinct purposes and caters to different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
