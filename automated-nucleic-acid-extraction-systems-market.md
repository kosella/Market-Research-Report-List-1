<p><p>Automated nucleic acid extraction systems are innovative technologies utilized in molecular biology laboratories to extract nucleic acids from samples efficiently and accurately. These systems can be categorized into two main types: fully automated and semi-automated. Fully automated systems perform the entire extraction process automatically, from sample handling to elution of nucleic acids, reducing the need for human intervention. In contrast, semi-automated systems require some manual steps, such as sample loading, but still automate the majority of the extraction procedure. Both types offer advantages in terms of speed, accuracy, and reproducibility for nucleic acid extraction.</p></p>
