<p><p>Medical heat shrink tubing is used in various medical applications, and it is available in different types. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) heat shrink tubing is a highly flexible and transparent material that offers excellent insulation and protection. It is commonly used for electrical insulation in medical devices and equipment. On the other hand, FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) heat shrink tubing provides exceptional chemical resistance and can withstand high temperatures. It is widely used in medical applications where resistance to chemicals and heat is crucial, such as catheter coating and wire harness protection.</p></p>
