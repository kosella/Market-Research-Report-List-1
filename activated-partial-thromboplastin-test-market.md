<p><p>The Activated Partial Thromboplastin Test (APTT) market includes three types of technologies for testing blood clotting time. Optical technology involves measuring the clotting process using light absorption or scattering. Mechanical technology assesses the clotting time by observing changes in pressure or impedance caused by clot formation. Electrochemical technology measures changes in electrical properties during clot formation. These different technologies provide healthcare professionals with multiple options to accurately assess blood clotting disorders and guide appropriate treatments.</p></p>
