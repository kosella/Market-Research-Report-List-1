<p><p>Skin Ulcer Anti-Infectives market types include Pressure Ulcer, Diabetic Ulcer, Venous Ulcer, and Arterial Ulcer. Pressure ulcers result from prolonged pressure on the skin, primarily seen in bedridden patients. Diabetic ulcers occur in individuals with diabetes due to poor circulation and nerve damage. Venous ulcers develop from impaired venous circulation in the legs. Arterial ulcers are caused by poor blood circulation in the arteries, commonly seen in individuals with peripheral artery disease. Each type requires specific anti-infective treatments tailored to address underlying causes.</p></p>
