<p><ul><li>Total Hip Systems</li><li>Partial Hip Systems</li><li>Revision Hip Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hip resurfacing implant market includes various applications such as total hip systems, partial hip systems, and revision hip systems. Total hip systems involve replacing the entire hip joint, including both the femoral head and the acetabulum. Partial hip systems involve replacing only the femoral head and retaining the acetabulum. Revision hip systems are designed for patients who have previously undergone hip replacement surgery and require a new implant. These different applications cater to the specific needs and conditions of patients who require hip resurfacing procedures.</p></p>
