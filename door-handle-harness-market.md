<p><p>The Door Handle Harness market comprises two main types: In-Door Wiring Harness and Under The Door Harness. In-Door Wiring Harness refers to the wiring system that is installed inside the door panel, connecting the door handle to the vehicle's electrical system. It provides power for functions like electric locks and windows. On the other hand, Under The Door Harness refers to the wiring system that is located beneath the vehicle's door, connecting the handle to the interior electrical system. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operation of door handle functionalities in vehicles.</p></p>
