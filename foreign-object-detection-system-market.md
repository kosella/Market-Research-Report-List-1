<p><p>The Foreign Object Detection System market includes three main types: Magnetic Separator, Metal Detector, and X-ray Machine. A Magnetic Separator is used to attract and remove magnetic contaminants from products in industries such as food processing and mining. A Metal Detector detects metallic objects by emitting electromagnetic waves and analyzing the reflected signals, commonly used in security screening. X-ray Machines use X-rays to scan and detect foreign objects inside products or packages, crucial for quality control in industries such as pharmaceuticals and electronics. These systems play a vital role in ensuring product safety and preventing hazards.</p></p>
