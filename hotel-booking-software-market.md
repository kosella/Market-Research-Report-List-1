<p><p>The hotel booking software market is classified into two types based on the deployment model: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based hotel booking software operates on remote servers and can be accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. On the other hand, premise-based hotel booking software is installed directly on the hotel's hardware and managed in-house. It provides more control over data and operations but requires higher upfront investments. Both types have their advantages and considerations, and the choice depends on the hotel's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
