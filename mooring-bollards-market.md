<p><p>The mooring bollards market comprises various types, including double bitt bollards, single bitt bollards, pillar bollards, and others. Double bitt bollards consist of two vertical posts connected by a crossbeam, offering secure mooring for large vessels. Single bitt bollards have a single vertical post, suitable for smaller boats and ships. Pillar bollards are designed with a cylindrical shape and provide excellent strength and stability. The "others" category includes different types of bollards, such as T-head bollards and cleat bollards, catering to specific mooring requirements in various industries.</p></p>
