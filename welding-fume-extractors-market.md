<p><p>Welding fume extractors are devices used to remove hazardous fumes and particles generated during welding processes. Stationary type extractors are permanently installed in a fixed location, typically in welding shops or factories, providing continuous extraction in a specific area. Both types serve the same purpose of ensuring a safe and healthy working environment by capturing and filtering welding fumes effectively.</p></p>
