<p><p>People Counting Software Market Types - IR Beam, Thermal Imaging, Video Based:</p><p>1) IR Beam: This type of people counting software utilizes Infrared (IR) beams to detect the presence of individuals. When the beam is interrupted by a person crossing its path, the system registers the count. This non-intrusive method is commonly used in areas with narrower entrances or exits.</p><p>2) Thermal Imaging: Thermal imaging-based software measures the body heat emitted by individuals to determine their presence and count. This technology is often installed in areas where accuracy is crucial, such as high-security zones or places with varying lighting conditions.</p><p>3) Video Based: Video-based people counting software uses cameras to capture and analyze footage, recognizing and tracking individuals. This type of software can be applied in various environments, providing a cost-effective and flexible solution. It typically utilizes advanced algorithms for accurate counting, even in complex scenarios.</p></p>
