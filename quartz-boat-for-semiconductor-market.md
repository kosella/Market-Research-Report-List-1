<p><p>The quartz boat for semiconductor market consists of two types: vertical quartz boats and horizontal quartz boats. Vertical quartz boats are designed to hold multiple wafers in a vertical position for processes such as etching and deposition. They provide efficient space utilization and easy handling of wafers. On the other hand, horizontal quartz boats are used for batch processing and are capable of holding larger quantities of wafers in a horizontal position. They are suitable for processes like annealing and diffusion. Both types play a critical role in the semiconductor industry's manufacturing processes.</p></p>
