<p><p>The thermal socks market consists of two main types: electric heated socks and non-electric heated socks. Electric heated socks are designed with built-in battery-powered heating elements, providing continuous warmth to the wearer. They typically offer adjustable heat levels and can be recharged. On the other hand, non-electric heated socks rely on insulating materials and natural body heat retention to keep feet warm. They often feature extra-thick layers or specialized fabrics to provide insulation and can be worn with regular footwear. Both types cater to individuals looking for added comfort and warmth in cold weather conditions.</p></p>
