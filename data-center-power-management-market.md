The rising adoption of technologies such as cloud computing, IoT, and big data analytics has led to the proliferation of data centers worldwide, resulting in a surge in power consumption.</p><p>Furthermore, the need for energy-efficient data centers is driving the adoption of power management solutions. Rising energy costs and environmental concerns have prompted data center operators to focus on reducing power usage and optimizing their energy consumption.
