<p><p>The normal headphone market consists of three main types: over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear headphones. Over-ear headphones feature large ear cups that encompass the ears, providing excellent sound quality and noise isolation. On-ear headphones have smaller ear cups that rest on the ears, offering portability and decent sound isolation. In-ear headphones, also known as earbuds, are small, directly inserted into the ear canals, and are highly portable. They provide good noise isolation and are commonly used for mobile listening. These types cater to different preferences and needs, offering a range of options for consumers.</p></p>
