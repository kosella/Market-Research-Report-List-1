<p><p>The aviation battery market comprises three main types: Nickel Cadmium (Ni-Cd), Lead Acid, and Lithium Ion batteries. Ni-Cd batteries are durable, with a longer life cycle, but contain toxic substances and suffer from memory effect. Lead Acid batteries are affordable and have good power output but are heavy and require regular maintenance. Lithium Ion batteries are lightweight, offer high energy density and longer lifespan, but are more expensive. Each type has its advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different aviation applications.</p></p>
