<p><p>Distribution Feeder Automation (DFA) refers to the automated monitoring and control of power distribution systems. The DFA market includes various types, such as Fault Location, Isolation, and Service Restoration (FLISR), and Automatic Transfer Scheme (ATS). FLISR systems quickly detect faults in the distribution network, isolate the affected area, and restore service to unaffected areas. ATS systems automatically transfer power supply from the primary source to an alternative source when a fault or outage is detected. These automation technologies enhance the reliability and efficiency of distribution systems.</p></p>
