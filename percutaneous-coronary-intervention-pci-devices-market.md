<p><p>Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) devices market includes various types of medical devices used in the treatment of coronary artery disease. Coronary stents are small mesh-like tubes placed in narrowed blood vessels to keep them open. PTCA catheters are used to inflate a balloon in narrowed arteries to improve blood flow. Coronary guidewires help guide the placement of these devices during procedures. Embolic protection devices are used to prevent debris from traveling to other parts of the body during the intervention. These devices collectively contribute to enhancing blood flow and managing coronary artery diseases.</p></p>
