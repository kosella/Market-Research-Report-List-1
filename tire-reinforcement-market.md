<p><p>Tire reinforcement is classified into four types: steel, polyester, rayon, and nylon. Steel reinforcement provides increased tire strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Polyester reinforcement offers good resistance against high temperatures and excellent adhesion properties. Rayon reinforcement provides a comfortable ride due to its flexibility and good traction. Nylon reinforcement offers high tensile strength and excellent resistance to deformation, and it is commonly used in passenger car tires. These different types of reinforcement materials cater to the diverse needs of the tire industry, ensuring enhanced performance and safety.</p></p>
