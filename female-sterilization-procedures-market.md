<p><p>Female sterilization procedures refer to permanent methods of contraception for women. There are different types of procedures available in the market. Tubal implant involves inserting devices into the fallopian tubes to block them. Tubal sealing involves using heat or a special material to close off the fallopian tubes. Tubal tying involves using surgical threads or clips to close off the fallopian tubes. Tubal ligation refers to the surgical procedure of cutting, tying, or sealing the fallopian tubes to prevent eggs from reaching the uterus, thus preventing pregnancy. These procedures are effective and offer long-term contraception options for women.</p></p>
