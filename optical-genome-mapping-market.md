<p><p>The optical genome mapping market comprises two main segments: consumables and instruments. Consumables refer to the materials and products necessary for performing optical genome mapping, such as reagents, kits, and arrays. These consumables are essential for DNA labeling and imaging processes. On the other hand, instruments include all the hardware, machinery, and equipment needed to conduct optical genome mapping, such as imaging systems, scanners, and sequencers.
