<p><p>Operating room (OR) management solutions refer to software systems that streamline and enhance the efficiency of workflows in the OR. There are two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise solutions require the installation and maintenance of software and hardware within the hospital's infrastructure, providing local control and security. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, accessible through the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. Both types aim to optimize OR processes, such as scheduling, patient tracking, resource allocation, and documentation, to improve patient care and operational outcomes.</p></p>
