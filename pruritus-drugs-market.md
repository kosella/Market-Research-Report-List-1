<p><p>Pruritus drugs market can be categorized into three forms: topical, oral, and parenteral. Topical medications are applied directly to the affected area of the skin to alleviate itching. Oral medications are taken by mouth and may include antihistamines, corticosteroids, or other types of drugs to relieve itching. Parenteral medications are administered through injections or infusions and can provide quick relief from severe itching, often used in more severe cases or when oral medications prove ineffective. These different forms cater to various levels of pruritus severity and offer different modes of administration for patient convenience.</p></p>
