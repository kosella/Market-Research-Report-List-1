<p><strong>The Seismic Simulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uniaxial Seismic Simulators</li><li>Biaxial Seismic Simulators</li><li>6DOF Seismic Simulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seismic simulators are categorized into three main types: uniaxial, biaxial, and 6DOF (six degrees of freedom). Uniaxial simulators replicate seismic motion in one direction, biaxial simulators simulate motion in two perpendicular directions, and 6DOF simulators provide motion in multiple directions to closely mimic real-world seismic events. Each type of simulator offers unique capabilities for testing structures, equipment, and materials under various seismic conditions, helping to improve the safety and resilience of infrastructure.</p></p>
