Vertebroplasty is another minimally invasive procedure where bone cement is directly injected into the fractured vertebrae. Other procedures in this market include radiofrequency kyphoplasty and spinal implant systems. These techniques help stabilize the fractured vertebrae and provide pain relief to patients suffering from vertebral compression fractures.</p></p>
