<p><p>The electric floor scrubber market consists of three main types: walk-behind floor scrubbers, ride-on floor scrubbers, and stand-on floor scrubbers. Walk-behind floor scrubbers are manually operated and require the user to walk behind the machine. Ride-on floor scrubbers are designed for larger areas and allow the operator to ride on the machine while cleaning. Stand-on floor scrubbers are similar to ride-on scrubbers but offer a smaller footprint, making them suitable for navigating tighter spaces. These different types cater to varying cleaning needs and surface areas.</p></p>
