Milk powder, on the other hand, involves drying milk and adding probiotic cultures to create a powdered form that can be easily consumed or incorporated into other products. Probiotic supplements are generally in the form of capsules or tablets, containing a concentrated dose of beneficial bacteria strains that can be taken as a dietary supplement.</p></p>
