<p><p>The network management market encompasses various types, each serving a specific purpose. Network traffic management involves monitoring and controlling the flow of data within a network, ensuring efficient performance and preventing congestion. Network equipment management focuses on overseeing and maintaining network devices, such as routers and switches, to ensure their optimal functionality. Network configuration management involves managing network settings, policies, and configurations to maintain consistency and conformity across the network. Lastly, network security management addresses safeguarding the network from unauthorized access, threats, and vulnerabilities, implementing measures like firewalls and encryption to mitigate risks.</p></p>
