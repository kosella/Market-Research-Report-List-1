<p><p>Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD) is a genetic disorder that causes fluid-filled cysts to develop in the kidneys. This condition can lead to various complications such as kidney stones, high blood pressure, and kidney failure. Polycystic kidney disease treatment aims to manage symptoms, slow the progression of the disease, and prevent complications.</p><p>The treatment options for PKD include medication, lifestyle changes, and dialysis or kidney transplantation in severe cases. Medications like ACE inhibitors or angiotensin receptor blockers may be prescribed to control high blood pressure and slow kidney damage. Lifestyle changes such as maintaining a healthy diet, quitting smoking, and regular exercise can also help manage symptoms.</p><p>In recent years, there has been significant growth in the Polycystic Kidney Disease Treatment Market.
