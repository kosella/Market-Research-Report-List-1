<p><p>The Hi-Flo Stopcock market offers different types of stopcocks designed for various medical applications. The 4-way stopcock provides four ports for multiple fluid inputs or outputs, allowing for efficient fluid management in medical procedures. The 3-way stopcock comes with three ports and is commonly used in arterial and venous pressure monitoring or in administering multiple medications. The 2-way stopcock offers two ports and is used for simple fluid control, while the 1-way stopcock has a single port and is primarily utilized for fluid infusion.</p></p>
