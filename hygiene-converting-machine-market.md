<p><p>The Hygiene Converting Machine market consists of two main types: Full-servo and Semi-servo machines. Full-servo machines are equipped with servo motors for each section of the machine. This allows for precise control over each operation, enhancing efficiency and reducing waste. On the other hand, Semi-servo machines only have a few servo motors, typically for major operations. While they offer some level of automation and control, they are generally less advanced and adaptable compared to full-servo machines.</p></p>
