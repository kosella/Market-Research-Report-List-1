DBC substrates use a ceramic material with a copper layer for better heat dissipation. AMB substrates possess high thermal conductivity due to active metal brazing. IMS substrates have insulating layers to improve thermal performance and isolation. Lastly, the "Others" category includes substrates made from materials like aluminum nitride or aluminum oxide, offering a range of benefits such as low cost or high thermal conductivity.</p></p>
