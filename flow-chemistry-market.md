<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The flow chemistry market encompasses different types of reactors used for continuous chemical processes. Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR) involve a well-mixed reaction mixture flowing in and out of the reactor. Plug Flow Reactors (PFR) maintain a narrow flow channel, allowing reactants to flow through in a plug-like manner. Micro Reactor Systems (MRT) consist of small-scale reactors with precisely controlled conditions. The "Others" category comprises various reactor designs that do not fit into the aforementioned categories, such as oscillatory flow reactors or fixed-bed reactors. These reactors offer advantages like improved efficiency, safety, and scalability in chemical reactions.</p></p>
