<p><strong>The Plasma Etching Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP)</li><li>Reactive Ion Etching (RIE)</li><li>Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma etching systems are used in the semiconductor industry for precise and controlled material removal processes. There are various types of plasma etching systems available in the market. </p><p>Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) is a type of plasma source that utilizes radiofrequency power to generate plasma, enabling efficient etching with minimal damage to the substrate. </p><p>Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) involves the reaction of gases with the material surface, where ions bombard the substrate for etching. It offers high selectivity and anisotropic etching.</p><p>Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) is a specialized form of RIE that enables deep etching with aspect ratios exceeding 20:1.</p><p>Other types of plasma etching systems include magnetically enhanced ion etching (MEIE) and electron cyclotron resonance etching (ECRE), among others.</p></p>
