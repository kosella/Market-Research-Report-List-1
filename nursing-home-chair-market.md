<p><p>The nursing home chair market consists of two main types: manual nursing home chairs and electric nursing home chairs. Electric nursing home chairs, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and offer advanced features such as motorized movement, adjustable seats, and built-in lifting mechanisms for easier transfers. These chairs are designed to enhance comfort and promote independence for elderly individuals in nursing homes.</p></p>
