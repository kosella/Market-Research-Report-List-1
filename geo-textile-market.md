<p><p>Geo-Textile Market is segmented into different types based on their construction and material composition. Nonwoven Geo-Textiles are made from synthetic fibers and are commonly used for filtration and separation applications. Woven Geo-Textiles are made by weaving together yarns and are known for their strength and durability. Knitted Geo-Textiles are produced by interlocking loops of yarn and are often used for drainage and reinforcement purposes. Other types of Geo-Textiles include composite Geo-Textiles, which combine characteristics of different types to cater to specific project requirements.</p></p>
